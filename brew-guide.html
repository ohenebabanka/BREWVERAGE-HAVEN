Master the art of brewing the perfect cup at home

‚òï Pour-Over Method

Equipment Needed:

  • Pour-over dripper (V60 or Chemex)
  • Paper filter
  • Gooseneck kettle
  • Fresh coffee (medium grind)
  • Scale and timer

Steps:

  1. Heat water to 96¬∞C
  2. Rinse filter with hot water
  3. Add 15g coffee (medium grind)
  4. Bloom with 30ml water for 30 seconds
  5. Pour slowly in circles to 250ml total
  6. Total brew time: 2:30 - 3 minutes

Steps:

  1. Add 30g coarse ground coffee
  2. Pour 350ml water at 94¬∞C

üßä Cold Brew

Equipment Needed:

  • Large jar or cold brew maker
  • Coarse ground coffee
  • Fine mesh strainer
  • Filter paper

Steps:

  1. Mix 100g coffee with 500ml cold water
  2. Stir to saturate all grounds
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 12-16 hours
  4. Strain through fine mesh
  5. Dilute 1:1 with water or milk
  6. Serve over ice

üí° Keeps for up to 2 weeks in fridge
